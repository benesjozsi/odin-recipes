Ingredients

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 Tbsp softened butter

Steps

  1. In a stand mixer, combine bread flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, salt and yeast.
  2. Add wet ingredients of water, milk, and egg.
  3. With the mixer running on medium low speed, add softened butter one scoop at a time until all of the butter is incorporated.
  4. Transfer dough to a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
  5. Once risen, divide dough into 8 equal portions and shape into balls.
  6. Place in the oven set to 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 15-20 minutes.
