Ingredients

  • 14 g instant yeast
  • 617g water - 95F
  • 950g all-purpose flour
  • 19g fine sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 25g granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and a baking steel or pizza stone at 500F for 45 minutes before utilizing it.
  2. Whisk yeast and sugar into your water, and allow it to sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Place your dough in a greased bowl, then cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 1 - 2 hours at room temperature or until double in size.
  4. Punch down your dough and dive into 300g pieces. Form those pieces into balls and place them in a floured proofing pizza box.
  5. In a medium sauce pot, add butter and olive oil.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and a splotch of olive oil, boil over medium-high, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 12 minutes.
